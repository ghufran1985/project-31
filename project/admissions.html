Admissions at Pakistan International School

Pakistan International School offers an easy and great admission process, ensuring that every student has an chance to join our academic.

Easy Admission Process

Our school offers a simple and strait admissions process, with just one entrance exam required. This exam is designed to see the basic knowledge and skills needed to succeed at PISQ.

Additionally we believe in offering flexibility to our students. We allow them to choose their subjects based on their personal interest . Students can select from a wide range of subjects to their desires. if they find that a particular subject is not the right fit they have the option to change it within the first month of the school year.

we conduct an interview with each student to better understand their background, goals, and motivations.
