Why Choose PISQ?

At PISQ, we are proud to have some of the brightest and most talented students in the region. Our students consistently achieve excellent results in exams.

We are one of the first Pakistani schools to establish in Qatar and over the 20+ years we have built a reputation for academic brillaince offering great education that is to meet international standards.

Our Cambridge curriculum allows our students to be great academically and be prepared for the challenges of the LIFE. Whether in Science Arts or Sports our students stand at the forefront of education in the region.
